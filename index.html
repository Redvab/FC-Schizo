OFFICIAL: KHABIB IS GOING TO DAGESTAN!

July 18, 2026

After a disastrous performance in his last match, FC Schizo management made a tough decision.

Our goalkeeper, Khabib, made several serious errors that directly impacted the final result. After the final whistle, the head coach didn't hide his disappointment, and the club promptly announced his "transfer" to Dagestan.

"We appreciate everything Khabib has done for the team, but after such a performance, it's best for him to take a short break. We wish him good luck in Dagestan," the club said in an official statement.

Fans are already actively discussing this decision on social media, while Khabib himself has not yet commented on the situation.

RENOVATION: Trentinerplatz will triple in size

July 18, 2026

FC Schizo management has officially announced a major renovation of its home stadium, Trentinerplatz.

Following numerous requests from fans and the club's rapid growth, the decision has been made to increase the arena's capacity from 15,000 to 45,000.

Construction work will begin shortly and will be the largest project in the club's history.

"Our fans deserve a world-class stadium. Trentinerplatz will become a true football fortress and will be able to accommodate three times as many fans," the club said in a statement.

Aleks Kanchev: Potential Bust or Future Club Legend?

July 13, 2026

As FC Schizo secures its historical promotion to Division 3, analysts and fans have begun questioning the overall impact of winger Aleksander Kanchev. While the Bulgarian talent brings undeniable speed and high individual flair to the flank, some critics are raising concerns about his tactical consistency on the big stage.

Labelled by some as a "potential bust" due to a couple of quiet games during the high-pressure promotion run, Kanchev still maintains the full backing of his teammates. "He's an explosive player. In Division 3, defenses are tighter, but Aleks has all the qualities to silence the doubters," Popic stated in a recent interview. Only time will tell if Kanchev will crack under pressure or prove to be the bargain of the season.
